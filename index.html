How it works

From photo to price in three steps.

01

Snap & scan

Photograph one card or a whole spread. The scanner reads the name, set code, number, and rarity off each card.

02

We verify it

Every card is checked against the live market database. Confident matches are marked verified; anything uncertain gets flagged for you to confirm.

03

Value or list

Get a total collection value with a buy-offer calculator, or generate eBay-ready listings — your choice, same scan.
